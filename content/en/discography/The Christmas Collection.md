# Notes

*The Christmas Collection* is a set of pieces that evoke the spirit of Christmas, not only through traditional carols or holiday songs, but through their uplifting mood, thoughtful orchestration, and timeless character. Each composition in this collection is meant to capture the warmth, joy, and nostalgia of the season, offering listeners a musical journey that feels both familiar and celebratory.
